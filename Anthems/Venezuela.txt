Gritaba el señor;

el vil egoísmo
que otra vez triunfó.

“Down with chains!”
Shouted the Lord; 
And the poor man in his hovel
For Freedom implored.
Upon this holy name
Trembled in great dread
The vile selfishness
That had once prevailed.

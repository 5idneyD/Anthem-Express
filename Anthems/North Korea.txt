Land of the Morning calm, 
full of sunshine, full of gold, silver,
This is the beautiful homeland 
of three thousand li of our brave people.
Honored by its five thousand-year-old history, 
the brilliance of its culture
May everyone devote themselves body and soul 
to our beloved Korea!

The spirit of Paektu animates us 
as much as the love of work,
Through united justice 
we want to walk at the head of the world,
A people brimming with energy 
who built their country,
May everyone to a Korea worthy 
and prosperous forever be honored!
